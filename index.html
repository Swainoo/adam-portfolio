Selected work

Four problems, handled.

A sample of the systems and automation work I deliver day to day.

CASE-01 Adopted

The case for a new ticketing platform

Problem Our internal ticketing tool was unstructured and gave the business almost no usable data — no consistent taxonomy, and very little visibility into ticket quality or volume.
What I did Researched the enterprise ticketing and case-management market, evaluated options against taxonomy and data fidelity, then presented leadership with a recommendation for standardising the IT experience, improving insight for our QA team, and lifting technician efficiency.

Became the foundation for our move to Salesforce Service Cloud, including the requirements and workflow specs that shaped how it was built.

CASE-02 Shipped

AI-powered project intake tool

Problem 400+ project ideas a year arrived through an unstructured process, with duplicate submissions and slow routing.
What I did Designed and built a full-stack AI application from scratch — no engineering support — using LLM-driven semantic search for duplicate detection, AI-generated one-pagers, and intelligent routing.

Already used by 300+ people, on track to handle 400+ submissions a year.

CASE-03 Ongoing

Running the continuous improvement pipeline

Problem Every enhancement request — through the intake tool or otherwise — needs its impact validated, prioritised against engineering capacity, and tested before release, or the backlog turns into noise instead of progress.
What I do Validate the business impact of projects submitted through my intake tool, work with engineering to prioritise what gets built next, then partner with business stakeholders on testing and release.

Runs as a continuous cycle — 29 production changes shipped through it in Q1 2026 alone.

CASE-04 In rollout

AI features for a 300-person helpdesk

Problem The CRM platform had real potential to cut technician workload, but no defined roadmap for getting there.
What I did Defined requirements for 10 AI features working directly with an external vendor on design and rollout planning.
